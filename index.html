Часи в англійській мові

Present Simple

Індикатори: always, usually, often, every day, never

Приклад: I always drink coffee in the morning.

Affirmative: Subject + V1 (verb)

Negative: Subject + do/does not + V1

Interrogative: Do/Does + Subject + V1?

Present Continuous

Індикатори: now, at the moment, currently

Приклад: I am reading a book right now.

Affirmative: Subject + am/is/are + V-ing

Negative: Subject + am/is/are not + V-ing

Interrogative: Am/Is/Are + Subject + V-ing?

Present Perfect

Індикатори: already, just, yet, ever

Приклад: I have already finished my homework.

Affirmative: Subject + have/has + V3 (past participle)

Negative: Subject + have/has not + V3

Interrogative: Have/Has + Subject + V3?

Present Perfect Continuous

Індикатори: for, since, all day, lately

Приклад: I have been working here for three years.

Affirmative: Subject + have/has been + V-ing

Negative: Subject + have/has not been + V-ing

Interrogative: Have/Has + Subject + been + V-ing?

Past Simple

Індикатори: yesterday, last week, two days ago

Приклад: I visited my grandmother last week.

Affirmative: Subject + V2 (past simple)

Negative: Subject + did not + V1

Interrogative: Did + Subject + V1?

Past Continuous

Індикатори: while, when, at 5 o'clock yesterday

Приклад: I was cooking dinner when you called.

Affirmative: Subject + was/were + V-ing

Negative: Subject + was/were not + V-ing

Interrogative: Was/Were + Subject + V-ing?

Future Simple

Індикатори: tomorrow, next week, soon

Приклад: I will visit my grandmother tomorrow.

Affirmative: Subject + will + V1

Negative: Subject + will not + V1

Interrogative: Will + Subject + V1?

Future Continuous

Індикатори: at this time tomorrow, in a week

Приклад: I will be traveling at this time tomorrow.

Affirmative: Subject + will be + V-ing

Negative: Subject + will not be + V-ing

Interrogative: Will + Subject + be + V-ing?

Future Perfect

Індикатори: by, by the time

Приклад: I will have finished my work by 6 o'clock.

Affirmative: Subject + will have + V3 (past participle)

Negative: Subject + will not have + V3

Interrogative: Will + Subject + have + V3?
